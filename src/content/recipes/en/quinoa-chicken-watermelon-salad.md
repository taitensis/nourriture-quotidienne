---
title: "Summer Quinoa Salad with Chicken & Watermelon"
date: "2025-08-27"
tags: ["salad", "summer"]
time: { prep: "20 min", cook: "15 min", total: "35 min" }
yield: "6 servings"
images:
  - { src: "/images/quinoa-chicken-watermelon-salad-hero.jpg", alt: "Colorful summer quinoa salad with watermelon and grilled chicken" }
ingredient_groups:
  - title: "Salad"
    ingredients:
      - "2 cups quinoa, rinsed"
      - "300 g feta cheese, crumbled"
      - "200 g cherry tomatoes, halved"
      - "2 cups watermelon, cubed"
      - "2 cooked chicken breasts, sliced"
      - "1 small red onion, finely sliced"
      - "1 small cucumber, diced"
      - "A handful of fresh mint or basil leaves"
  - title: "Dressing"
    ingredients:
      - "3 tbsp olive oil"
      - "1 tbsp lemon juice"
      - "1 tbsp white wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar"
      - "Salt and pepper, to taste"
steps:
  - "Cook the quinoa in salted water according to package instructions. Let it cool completely."
  - "Prepare the dressing by whisking together olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, and pepper."
  - "In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, chicken, watermelon, and herbs."
  - "Drizzle with the dressing and toss gently to combine."
  - "Top with crumbled feta and serve chilled or at room temperature."
notes:
  - "You can substitute grilled halloumi or chickpeas for a vegetarian option."
  - "Add arugula or baby spinach for extra greens."
  - "Keeps well in the fridge for up to 2 days."
---
