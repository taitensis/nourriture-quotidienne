ingredient_groups:
  - ingredients:
      - '1 medium zucchini, sliced'
      - '1 capsicum, sliced'
      - '1 carrot, coarsely grated'
      - '200 g firm tofu, pressed & cubed'
      - 1 tbsp cornstarch (for tofu)
      - 2 tbsp neutral oil (e.g. canola or peanut)
      - '2 cloves garlic, minced'
      - 1 tbsp grated ginger (optional)
      - 1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
      - 2 tbsp soy sauce
      - 1 tbsp lime juice
      - 1 tsp sugar
      - 1 tsp sesame oil (for finishing)
steps:
  - >-
    Pat tofu dry, cube it, toss in cornstarch. Pan-fry in oil until golden, and
    set aside.
  - 'In the same wok, sauté garlic and ginger for 30 sec. to 1 min.'
  - >-
    Add five-spice, stir briefly. Then add zucchini and capsicum, and stir-fry
    for 2 to 3 min.
  - Add grated carrot and cook another 2 min. until veggies are just tender.
  - >-
    Return tofu to the wok. Add soy sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Stir to coat
    evenly.
  - 'Drizzle with sesame oil, toss everything well, and serve hot.'
notes:
  - Serve over rice or noodles.
  - Add chili flakes or chili oil if you like heat.
  - Use mushrooms or snap peas for variety.
