ingredient_groups:
  - ingredients:
      - 250 g plain flour
      - 1 tsp baking powder
      - 1 tsp ground cinnamon
      - 300 g grated carrots
      - 180 g light brown sugar
      - 120 ml neutral oil
      - 1 tsp vanilla extract
      - 60 g walnuts, chopped
steps:
  - Preheat the oven to 180 °C. Grease a 24 cm pan.
  - Mix the flour, baking powder, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  - Stir in the grated carrots and brown sugar.
  - Add the oil and vanilla, then mix until just combined.
  - Fold in the walnuts and pour the batter into the pan.
  - Bake for about 30 min, until a skewer comes out clean.
  - Cool before slicing.
notes:
  - Add a cream cheese frosting for a classic finish.
  - Replace walnuts with pecans or hazelnuts if preferred.
  - Keeps well for 2–3 days in an airtight container.
