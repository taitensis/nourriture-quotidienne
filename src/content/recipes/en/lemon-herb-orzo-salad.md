---
title: "Lemon-Herb Orzo Salad"
date: "2025-08-27"
tags: ["salad", "orzo", "vegan", "quick"]
time: { prep: "10 min", cook: "10 min", total: "20 min" }
yield: "3 servings"
images:
  - { src: "/images/lemon-herb-orzo-hero.jpg", alt: "Bowl of lemony orzo salad" }
ingredient_groups:
  - title: "Salad"
    ingredients:
      - "200g orzo"
      - "1 small cucumber, diced"
      - "10 cherry tomatoes, halved"
      - "Handful parsley & mint, chopped"
      - "2 tbsp olive oil"
      - "1 lemon: zest + juice"
      - "Salt & pepper"
steps:
  - "Cook orzo until just tender; drain and cool under cold water."
  - "Toss with cucumber, tomatoes, herbs, olive oil, lemon zest & juice."
  - "Season to taste. Serve cool or at room temp."
notes:
  - "Add olives or chickpeas for extra heft."
