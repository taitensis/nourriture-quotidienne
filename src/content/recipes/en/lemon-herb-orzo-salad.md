title: Lemon-Herb Orzo Salad
date: 2025-08-27
lang: en
yield: 3 servings
difficulty: easy
tags:
  - vegan
  - salad
time:
  prep: 10 min
  cook: 10 min
  total: 20 min
images:
  - src: "/images/lemon-herb-orzo-hero.jpg"
    alt: "Bowl of lemony orzo salad"
ingredient_groups:
  - ingredients:
      - 200 g orzo
      - 1 small cucumber, diced
      - 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
      - Parsley & mint, chopped
      - 2 tbsp olive oil
      - 1 lemon (zest + juice)
      - Salt & pepper
steps:
  - >-
    Cook orzo until just tender; drain and cool under cold water.
  - >-
    Toss with cucumber, tomatoes, herbs, olive oil, lemon zest & juice.
  - >-
    Season to taste. Serve cool or at room temp.
notes:
  - Add olives or chickpeas for extra heft.
translation_key: lemon-orzo-salad
